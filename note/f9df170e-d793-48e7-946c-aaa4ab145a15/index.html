Wikilinks

🎋 Last Updated: 2021-05-08

[[Wikilinks]] “wikilink” actually goes by many names: “bidirectional link”, “wikitext linking”, “internal link”, “humble double bracket internal link”, to name only a few… are are crucial part of the world of digital gardening. They are the basic building block of the interlinking accomplished in many gardens, including this one.

Notable

  • Wikilinks are green As opposed to blue external links. and may be previewed on hover.
  • This type of linking is what defines the links in the net web graph type.
  • [[Wikilinks]] are rendered by the jekyll-wikilinks plugin.

Influences

  • digital garden jekyll template, initial d3 implementation in jekyll.

Proposal

  • Would jekyll or kramdown be interested in integrating this plugin?
