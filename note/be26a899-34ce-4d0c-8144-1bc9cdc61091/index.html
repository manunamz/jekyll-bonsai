Bonsai

🎋 Last Updated: 2021-05-13

Bonsai, literally “tray planting”, is the art of recreating a realistic picture of nature read as: the internet. in miniaturized form. From the perspective of a site-creator, they are seeding and pruning a jekyll-bonsai in a small tray-like space. The finer details are described well by the wikipedia article on bonsai:

Purposes of bonsai are primarily contemplation for the viewer, and the pleasant exercise of effort and ingenuity for the grower. By contrast with other plant cultivation practices, bonsai is not intended for production of food or for medicine read as: “data” or “engagement”. . Instead, bonsai practice focuses on long-term cultivation and shaping of one or more small trees growing in a container.”

…

A bonsai is created beginning with a specimen of source material. This may be a cutting, seedling 🌰 , or small tree 🎍 of a species suitable for bonsai development.

…

Bonsai uses cultivation techniques like pruning, root reduction, potting, defoliation, and grafting to produce small trees that mimic the shape and style of mature, full-size trees.
