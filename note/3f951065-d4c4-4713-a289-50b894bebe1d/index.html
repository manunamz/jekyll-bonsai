Graph

🎋 Last Updated: 2021-05-08

The graph in the sidebar is the main site navigation for this template. Just click on a node and you’ll be taken to it’s corresponding note. If you hover over the node, it’s title will display as a tooltip. You can also click the 🌳 or 🕸 to toggle the graph’s type.

You could think of the graph as a bonsai growing in the sidebar tray.

Notable Quirks

  • Graph gravity behavior is somewhat finicky – it is likely its numerical values will need to be tinkered with directly to make your graph fit the sidebar.

Influences
